Silence should not be a risk factor.

Most consumer safety systems rely on verbal communication, smartphone access, and multi-step interaction. In high-stress, medical, or nonverbal environments, these assumptions fail the people who need help most.

Guardian context, without surveillance.

A caregiver-controlled companion for wearable status, emergency context, and first responder access.

WallyVault is not a tracking app. No raw audio leaves the device. No video is uploaded to the cloud. Only event patterns and metadata alerts — time, location, trigger — reach the caregiver and authorized responders. The person wearing Wally Guard stays protected. Not surveilled.

Closed-Loop Safety Infrastructure.

01 · Detect

On-device TinyML processing. Heart rate, motion, sound — analyzed locally. No cloud required.

02 · Alert

Metadata alert (time + location + trigger) sent to caregiver device and authorized scanners. Raw data stays on device.

03 · Confirm

Closed-loop confirmation ensures the alert was received. Silence is not an answer.

Built for the most vulnerable.

  • Nonverbal individuals with autism, cognitive disabilities, or communication barriers.
  • Caregivers and families who need context, not cameras.
  • First responders — NFC/QR scan delivers: NON-VERBAL VULNERABLE — STOP, THINK, OBSERVE.
  • Schools — metadata alert displays HIGH DISTRESS on on-site scanner.
  • Emergency rooms — HIGH DISTRESS — NON-VERBAL PATIENT on ER scanner.
