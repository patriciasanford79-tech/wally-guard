About MyWallyBot LLC

MyWallyBot LLC is a South Carolina company, founded 2026, behind the WallyBot™ family of privacy-first safety, assistive technology, digital resource, and records-first advocacy properties.

What the Company Does

An umbrella for related public-facing properties.

MyWallyBot LLC develops and organizes public-facing properties connected by a shared focus on privacy-first infrastructure, practical safety design, documentation, and access. The company's work spans in-development assistive technology concepts, digital resource creation, and records-first advocacy tools. Each property has a distinct audience and purpose, but the umbrella structure keeps the work aligned around responsible design, clear documentation, and public-facing credibility.

Operating Principles

How the work gets organized.

Privacy by design

Build from the assumption that sensitive systems should collect less, expose less, and explain more.

Infrastructure before hype

Prioritize clear systems, credible documentation, and durable workflows over trend-driven product claims.

Plain-language access

Create tools and resources that make complex processes easier to understand without oversimplifying risk.

Records-first accountability

Treat documentation, timelines, and records access as practical infrastructure for families, advocates, and institutions.

Caregiver-informed development

Use lived operational experience as a development input while building for broader populations and system-level needs.

Responsible automation

Use automation to reduce friction, not to replace human judgment in legal, medical, safety, or regulatory contexts.

Founder & CEO

Tricia Sanford

MyWallyBot LLC was founded by Tricia Sanford, Founder & CEO, to organize a growing family of privacy-first tools and public-facing resources. The work is informed by caregiver experience, operational problem-solving, and a broader need for systems that make safety, documentation, access, and coordination easier to manage without relying on surveillance-heavy design.
